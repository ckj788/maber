FAQ

Does OMNIORA predict the future?

No. It describes patterns and timing windows so you can make better choices.

Why only 1–9?

We reduce composite numbers to their digital root (Pythagorean reduction). This preserves structure and keeps readings clear.

Is my data stored?

By default, form inputs are processed in your browser and not stored on our servers.

How is OMNIORA different from traditional numerology or astrology?

OMNIORA is grounded in Western numerology, so the way we compute your Life Number is the same: we use your date of birth to derive a number that reflects your personal path. But we don’t stop at labels. We blend insights from both Eastern and Western traditions, emphasize psychological layers, and focus on practical application in modern life.

Instead of offering a few lines of generic traits, we explore the “why” behind your pattern—childhood imprints, family dynamics, and subconscious beliefs—and then translate that insight into the “how”: concrete, real-world steps you can use to change habits and outcomes.

Compared with astrology, OMNIORA is more accessible and easier to apply. Astrology typically requires your exact birth time and place and involves complex chart calculations; OMNIORA only needs your date of birth, and your reading is unaffected even if you don’t know the exact time. Simple doesn’t mean shallow—numbers carry depth. Think of it this way: astrology reads a sky chart; Life Code reads a number map. They’re complementary mirrors of the self, and many users benefit from consulting both.

Will my Life Code ever change?

No. Your Life Code is derived from your birth date and functions like a “soul blueprint,” so it remains constant throughout life. What changes is how you express that energy as you grow. For example, a Life 1 may begin by proving themselves and later mature into service-oriented leadership. OMNIORA treats your number as a starting point for self-understanding, not a fixed destiny—your choices and actions still decide the path.

What if my total adds up to 11 or 22 (Master Numbers)?

Some numerology traditions treat 11, 22 (and sometimes 33) as Master Numbers and do not reduce them. OMNIORA currently interprets through the simplified 1–9 system. That means 11 is read primarily through the lens of 2 (1+1=2), and 22 through 4 (2+2=4), and so on.

We don’t ignore the nuance of Master Numbers. If your Life Code originates from 11 or 22, your report will note the intensified qualities often associated with them—for example, 11’s intuition or 22’s builder/manifestor capacity—layered onto the core number. Advanced content that dives deeper into Master Numbers is on our roadmap.
