Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-09-01

Our Philosophy

Welcome to OMNIORA. Your trust is the foundation of our work. Our privacy policy is simple: we collect the absolute minimum data required to generate your personalized report, and we do not store your personal report data after it's created. This document explains the details.

Who We Are

OMNIORA (“we,” “us”) offers numerology-based content and tools for self-discovery (our “Services”). For any questions about this policy, please reach out to us at support@omniora.xyz.

The Data We Process

To create your personalized report, we process two types of information: Information You Provide (Your name, date of birth, and email address) and Transactional Data (handled by our payment processor, PayPal; we do not see or store your credit card information).

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to fulfill your request. Your birth details are used once for the calculation and then immediately discarded. Your email address is used once to deliver your purchased PDF report. We will not send you marketing emails unless you explicitly consent.

Data Sharing

We do not, and will never, sell your personal data. The only service provider who handles your data is our payment processor for the purpose of completing your transaction. We do not share your personalization data (name, date of birth) with them.

Our Policy Regarding Children

Our Services are accessible to users of all ages. However, to make a purchase, you must be at least 18 years old or have the permission of a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 in compliance with privacy regulations.

Data Storage & Retention

Our core privacy promise is that we do not store your personal report data. The information you enter is processed transiently and is not saved in our database. The only information we may retain for customer support purposes is your Order ID and the email address used for delivery.

Your Rights & Choices

As we do not retain your personal report data, there is no data for us to provide access to, correct, or delete after your report is generated. For questions about your order, please contact us with your Order ID.

Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies to ensure the basic functionality of our website. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Our website contains links to our payment processor (PayPal). Any information you provide to them is governed by their own privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

How to Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please email us at support@omniora.xyz.
